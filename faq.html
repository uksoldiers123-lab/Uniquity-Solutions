Frequently Asked Questions

Payments

How do I make a payment?
Visit the Make a Payment section, enter your details and amount, then choose PayPal, card (via Stripe), or bank transfer. A confirmation will be sent once the payment is completed.
What payment methods are accepted?
We support PayPal, major credit cards through Stripe, and manual bank transfers. For alternative methods, please contact us.
When will my payment show as complete?
PayPal and card payments are confirmed within minutes. Bank transfers may take 1–3 business days based on your bank's processing time.
Do I get a receipt?
Yes, a receipt will be emailed to you after checkout. If you need additional copies, please contact our Support Center with your invoice/order number.

Merchants

How do I create a merchant account?
Use the Create Account (Merchants) section to submit your company details and KYC documents. Our team will assist you with onboarding and compliance.
How long does onboarding take?
Onboarding typically takes 2–5 business days, depending on the industry and completeness of documentation.
When are funds settled to merchants?
Standard settlement cycles range from T+2 to T+5 business days. Custom schedules may be arranged for eligible merchants.

Fees

What are your processing fees?
Processing fees vary by payment method, volume, and risk profile. Please visit our Pricing page or contact Sales for a customized quote.
Are there chargeback fees?
Chargebacks may incur a fee for each dispute, as detailed in your merchant agreement. Our team is available to assist with representment.

Security

How is my data protected?
We utilize industry-standard encryption, PCI DSS-compliant processes, and conduct regular security audits to safeguard your data.
Where can I find security documents?
Visit our Compliance & Security page for policies, certifications, and incident response information.

Support

How do I contact support?
You can reach us via the Support Center, live chat, or email. Response times may vary based on your plan and current demand.
Where can I find the Service Level Agreement (SLA)?
The SLA is available on our website under the Terms and Conditions section. If you have specific questions, please reach out to support.
